Mapo Tofu Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili bean paste
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 green onions, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Press tofu to remove excess water and cut it into cubes.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or pan, saut√© garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  3. Add ground pork and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in soy sauce, chili bean paste, chicken broth, and sugar.
  5. Gently add tofu cubes and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle green onions on top and serve hot.
